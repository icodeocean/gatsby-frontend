Coding Competitions

We offer programming competitions between private schools in the local Fairfield, Connecticut area. We are rapidly expanding to other highschool and college areas including New York, Massachusets, and Rhode Island.

Open Competitions

Each league inside of the Fairfield Programming Association allows for open competition. This means that every school is given the opportunity to compete, as long as they are within the age limit and qualify under all of the rules.

Ambassador Meetings

The Fairfield Programming Association holds meetings with two ambassadors from each school. The meetings are held in order to organize competitions, certify results, and create new rules. Each ambassador must be elected from their school to participate but there are no other requirements.
